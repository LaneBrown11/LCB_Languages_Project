Ruby

Basic Information

Created by Yukihiro Matsumoto, AKA Matz, in 1993

Designed as a simple, easy to use scripting language. Matz emphasized the enjoyability
of it, saying it would "help every programmer in the world to be productive, enjoy
programming, and be happy". It became a very popular language in Japan in the
late 90s/early 2000s, beating out Python in popularity. Its main use is for web development,
and is used by Apple, Twitter, Hulu, and more.

It's reputation for being a simple and enjoyable
programming experience still holds strong today.
